C&H Controls is an electrical and controls engineering company providing practical site-based services to industrial facilities.

We specialise in the installation, modification, testing, and commissioning of electrical control and instrumentation systems, with a focus on brownfield and operating plant environments.

Capabilities

Electrical Control Systems

Installation, modification, and upgrading of electrical control systems, including control panels, MCC interfaces, and field wiring.

Instrumentation

Installation, replacement, and modification of process instrumentation, including loop wiring and testing support.

Control Panels

Panel wiring, upgrades, fault rectification, and documentation updates for new and existing panels.

Breakdown & Fault Finding

Electrical and controls fault finding on industrial plant, including breakdown support and corrective works.

Testing & Commissioning

Loop checking, point-to-point testing, functional testing, and on-site commissioning support.

Brownfield Modifications

Electrical and controls modifications on live sites, including shutdown and staged upgrade works.
