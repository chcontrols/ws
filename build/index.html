C&H Controls

Electrical & Controls Engineering

C&H Controls is an electrical and controls engineering company providing practical site‑based services to industrial facilities.

We specialise in the installation, modification, testing, and commissioning of electrical control and instrumentation systems, with a focus on brownfield and operating plant environments.

Capabilities

  • Electrical Control Systems Installation, modification, and upgrading of electrical control systems, including control panels, MCC interfaces, and associated field wiring.
  • Instrumentation Installation & Modification Installation, replacement, and reconfiguration of process instrumentation, including loop wiring, terminations, and testing support.
  • Control Panel Works Control panel wiring, upgrades, fault rectification, and documentation updates for new and existing panels.
  • Breakdown Assistance & Fault Finding Electrical and controls fault finding on industrial plant and equipment, including breakdown support, issue isolation, and corrective works to restore safe operation.
  • Testing & Commissioning Loop checking, point‑to‑point testing, functional testing, and on‑site commissioning support during installations and upgrades.
  • Brownfield Modifications Electrical and controls modifications to existing plant, including shutdown works and staged upgrades on live sites.
